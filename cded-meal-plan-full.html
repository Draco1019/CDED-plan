Crohn's Disease Exclusion Diet

Your CDED Meal Plan

All 3 phases  ·  Weekly grocery lists  ·  Calorie tracking  ·  Synced with your infliximab schedule

💉 Infliximab (Remicade): Every 8 weeks  ·  Days marked on each week where your infusion falls. Rest, hydrate, and eat gently around infusion days.
Phase 1 (Weeks 1–6): Most restrictive. Lean proteins, white rice, white potato, peeled low-fiber fruits and vegetables only. No dairy, gluten, red meat, legumes, or processed foods.
